Bonobo

Along with the common chimpanzee, the bonobo is the closest extant relative to humans.

  • Scientific Name: Pan paniscus
  • Average Length: 80 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: unknown
  • Habitat: Congo Basin

The bonobo is distinguished by relatively long legs, pink lips, dark face and tail-tuft through adulthood, and parted long hair on its head.
